What is Open Schooling?

Open Schooling is a flexible learning system designed for school dropouts, working professionals, and students who need an alternative to traditional schooling. With BOSSE (Sikkim) and NIOS (National Board), students can complete their Secondary (Class X) or Senior Secondary (Class XII) education without the pressure of regular school.

This system is ideal for:

  • Students who had to leave formal education
  • Working professionals who want to complete their education
  • Students pursuing sports or arts who need flexible timing
  • Those who need a second chance to improve their scores
  • Learners in remote areas with limited access to schools

Boards We Offer

BOSSE

Board of Open Schooling & Skill Education, Sikkim

State open board based in Sikkim

BOSSE is a recognized open schooling board that provides flexible education opportunities to students across Sikkim and India.

Key Features:

  • Recognised for Class X & XII education
  • Offers flexible exam schedules
  • Skill-based learning integrated with academics
  • Ideal for students in Sikkim and neighboring regions
  • Affordable fee structure

NIOS

National Institute of Open Schooling

India's largest open school under Govt. of India

NIOS is a national board that provides quality education through open and distance learning模式.

Key Features:

  • Secondary (10th) & Senior Secondary (12th) programs
  • Wide range of subject choices
  • Comprehensive online study materials
  • Nationwide recognition and acceptance
  • Flexible examination system

Programs Available

Secondary (Class X)

For students who couldn't complete school or want to upgrade their qualification. This program provides a recognized Class X certificate equivalent to any other education board.

Senior Secondary (Class XII)

For students aiming for higher studies, jobs, or competitive exams. Complete your Class XII education with flexible learning options and choose from various streams.
